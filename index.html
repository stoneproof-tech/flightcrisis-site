How it works

From news to price prediction in seconds

1

AI reads the news

Every 15 minutes, our AI analyzes geopolitical headlines, official statements from world leaders, OPEC decisions, and oil price movements. Presidential declarations are weighted 3x more than generic news.

2

Calibrates against real data

Cross-checks estimates against IATA, Deutsche Bank, IEA, EIA, S&P Global, and Lloyd's of London. If our AI says +30% but IATA says +9%, it explains why.

3

Personalizes for your flight

Applies 7 factors specific to your route: which airline, where it refuels, how far the flight is, how exposed the carrier is to Gulf oil, and whether Hormuz blockade affects your route.

4

Shows 3 scenarios

Stalemate (war stays at current level), Escalation (destruction grows), Total War (Hormuz blocked, everything destroyed). Each with a timeline showing price evolution over time.

How it works

Three steps to smarter booking

FlightCrisis connects real-time geopolitical intelligence with flight pricing models.

Search your flight

Enter origin, destination, and date. We find real-time prices from 400+ airlines via Skyscanner, Kiwi, and eDreams.

AI analyzes the crisis

Our AI reads breaking news, tracks Hormuz Strait traffic, monitors jet fuel prices, and evaluates insurance premiums — every 15 minutes.

See the impact on your price

For each flight you get three scenario projections (Stalemate, Escalation, Total War) with timeline bars, recovery estimates, and booking advice.
